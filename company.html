Who We Are

Introduction -

H & A is a Custom T-shirt Printing Company and Daily Attire Manufacturer located in Kuantan, Pahang. H&A was established on July 2016 founded by Husna Athirah. Our product range includes Ready-made T-shirts such as Round Neck T-shirts, Collar-T-shirts and Polo-T-shirts and others customised T-shirts. Besides, we are also provided other product such as bag, jackets, jeans and other daily attire.

Our Service

Services-

T-shirt printing involves various complex techniques like cutting fabric, Screen Printing, Embroidery to print unique customised look that fulfill the customer need and expectation. H&A as new formed business is aim in being a leading of customised T-shirt supplier provides quality and exclusive product and services to our customer. Also, H&A want to be one of your precious daily needs.
